# SKILL: METHODOLOGY_ENGINE
- **Task:** Perform a "Technical Audit" of all research data found.
- **Criteria:** 
    1. Check for **Sample Size**: Is the data based on a small group or a massive study?
    2. Check for **Bias**: Who funded the research? Is there a conflict of interest?
    3. **Correlation vs. Causation**: Identify if Claude is assuming A caused B without proof.
    4. **The "Gold Standard"**: Prioritize Meta-analyses and Peer-Reviewed journals over blog posts or press releases.
- **Output:** Include a "Methodology Note" at the end of every report explaining the reliability of the sources.
