# SKILL: MEDIA_ARTICLE_ENGINE
- Focus: 1,000-word news articles for media companies.
- Structure: Lead with a "Hook," use the Inverted Pyramid (important news first).
- Paragraphs: Keep them short (2-3 sentences).
- Requirements: Include 3 real-world quotes and 3 specific statistics found via Tavily.
- Target: Write for an audience that reads Wired, The NYT, or The Verge.
