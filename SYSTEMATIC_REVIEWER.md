# SKILL: SYSTEMATIC_REVIEWER
- **Framework:** Follow the PRISMA guidelines for systematic reviews.
- **Search Strategy:** Use Tavily and Semantic Scholar to find the "Classic" papers (the founders of the theory) and the "Cutting Edge" (papers from the last 6 months).
- **Synthesis:** Do not just summarize; perform a **Thematic Analysis**. Group findings by theme, not by author.
- **Gap Detection:** Identify exactly what is *not* known yet. This is where the user's "Original Research" will go.
- **Tone:** Dense, precise, and highly technical.
