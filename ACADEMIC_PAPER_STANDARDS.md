# SKILL: ACADEMIC_PAPER_STANDARDS
- Focus: Formal research papers and reports.
- Structure: Abstract, Introduction, Methodology, Results, Discussion, Conclusion.
- Tone: Objective, technical, and formal. No slang or "hype."
- Citations: Use APA 7 format. Only use verified data from Semantic Scholar.
- Logic: Ensure all claims are backed by data; identify limitations in the research.
